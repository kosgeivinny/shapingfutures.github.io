Sanitary Towel Donation Program

Our Sanitary Towel Donation Program aims to ensure that young girls have access to sanitary products, helping to keep them in school and promoting menstrual health education.

What We Offer

  • Free sanitary towels to girls in need.
  • Workshops on menstrual health and hygiene.
  • Support for schools to provide these resources regularly.
  • Awareness campaigns to reduce stigma around menstruation.
