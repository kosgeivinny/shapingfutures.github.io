Mtoto na Elimu Program

Our Mtoto na Elimu program is dedicated to supporting students in their educational journey. We support students, waive fees for those in need, and regularly visit schools to monitor academic progress.

Support Offered

  • Waiving of school fees for underprivileged students.
  • Regular school visits to ensure academic progress.
  • Personalized mentorship and guidance.
